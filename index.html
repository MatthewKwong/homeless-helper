    Hello!

    We strive to help homeless people by connecting them with local resources and access to basic necessities, such as food.

    Through this application, users like you can notify locals of where homeless people are located, and we will send out a notification to the nearby users letting them know that there is someone nearby that needs help.

    We hope that this web application can help homeless people by providing them access to basic necessities.

    Thank you!
